Jan Nylén

Frontend developer and UX-designer

Hi, I am a UX-engineer from Northern Sweden. In 2017 I got my masters degree in Engineering in Interaction Technology and Design at Umea University. Since finishing my studies I have been working at CGI with UI and UX in a cross functional product team.

Contact me here or check out some of my projects if you want to get in touch.

Selected Projects

CGI 2018-2021

Customer Support Portal

As a frontend developer and UX-Designer I worked with designing new features for a customer portal and implementing them with Knockout.js and vue.js. Throughout 2019 I also took the lead on a UI/UX revamp of the portal. The purpose was to improve upon the problems that had been discovered since the first version of the UI was released.

A set of problem areas to focus on were defined and a design process was started to iteratively reach a new design with the help of prototypes and user tests. Most of the initial ideation and UX-work was based on previous support issues and a series of UX interviews held at the start of the year.

CGI 2020

DevOps Web App

As a UX-Designer I designed and prototyped a devOps web application. The application’s purpose was to give clients an overview and point of entry for a set of services hosted and offered to clients

During the project I interviewed members of the service teams regarding the functionality and purpose of each of their services, worked on defining their user base, and iteratively designed and created a high-fidelity prototype in Figma to act as a reference for the team during development.

Master Thesis

Visualizing Neuroscientific Data

Based on my interest for psychology and especially cognition I looked for a master thesis close to cognitive sciences. I got the opportunity to work together with a neuro cognitive research group to explore how to achieve an efficient and user friendly way of working with the multitude of datasets that their research generated. During the thesis I studied their data, iteratively interviewed the researchers, developed visualization prototypes and performed think aloud user tests.

SVT-play

Watching TV Together

In a course on prototype development for mobile applications we worked together with swedish public television (SVT) on a project to redesign their website SVT-play based on the way that television would be watched in the future. With our final concept and hi-fi prototype we combined the previous mobile web application with built-in communication services to allow for watching TV together without the need of being at the same place.

MillVid

Webbased Videogeneration

During the MillVid-project me and some fellow students worked together with Umea based IT-company Codemill to create an interactive webservice for small companies to use. We prepared an interview questionnaire, interviewed various employees at a few local companies, and went through several design iterations based on the questionnaire results. Thereafter we explored and developed a web based prototype and user interface for creating productvideos from uploaded images and javascript animations.
