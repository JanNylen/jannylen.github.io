Customer Support Portal

As a frontend developer and UX-Designer I worked with designing new features for a customer portal and implementing them with Knockout.js and vue.js. CGI 2022

MFA-Configurator

Multi-Factor-Authentication (MFA) has spread like wildfire and have in its wake made our digital life a safer place. Albeit, at the same time as securing our data it has also increased the complexity of user onboarding processes.

I spent most of my time on this project gaining an understanding of the intricacies of MFA through interviews, articles, looking at what other similar solutions have shared about their MFA-journeys, and thereafter outlining what kind of impact it would have on our onboarding.

After defining the pain points during the initial reaseach phase, I took figma in my hands and created a number of concepts. Testing and tweaking with colleagues through the processes and in the end creating a full out hi-fi prototype.

The most interesting findings throughout this process was actually how tentative and foreboding the support team was about the introduction of MFA. An at the end hearing their relief on how relatively pain free the introduction in the end was. While I hope that some of this was due to our design process I also have to give credit to the users. Despite the possible annoyance they were willing, and very understanding of this new security measure.

CGI 2020

DevOps Web Application

As a UX-Designer I designed and prototyped a devOps web application. The application’s purpose was to give clients an overview and point of entry for a set of services hosted and offered by my company.

During the project I interviewed members of the service teams regarding the functionality and purpose of each of their services, worked on defining their user base, and iteratively designed and created a high-fidelity prototype in Figma to act as a reference for the team during development.

Based on my interest for psychology and especially cognition I looked for a master thesis close to cognitive sciences. What I Specialize in

I have several years of experience as a UX-designer and frontend lead on a large scale B2B solution together with a cross-functional team. All the while moving from communicating with stakeholders, improving and adpating to needs, reviewing existing functionality, and aligning existing and new features with each other.

My path has given me a pragmatic and practical understanding of what it means to maintain a web-based application. From maintaining a design system and component based architecture, to accruing and scraping away design and technical debt, enabling efficient support processes, the impact of reliable (as well as unreliable...) tests, and most importantly; valuing iterations. A product is never perfect, but iterations inevitably brings you closer.
